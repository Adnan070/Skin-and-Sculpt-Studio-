Post-Operative Lymphatic Drainage Treatment

Post Operative Lymphatic Drainage is Essential for Patients who've recently undergone surgeries such as Tummy Tuck, BBL, Breast Augmentation & Liposuction. Treatment can begin as soon as 24 hrs post operation, with approval of the doctor & as long as there are no complications.

Heal Faster

Our Lymphatic drainage treatment helps promote tissue regeneration & reduce the severity of scars.  The most significant benefit of lymphatic drainage after an operation is to prevent or alleviate swelling allowing skin to adhere to avoid sagging and unwanted wrinkling.

Laser Lipo/Ilipo Body Sculpting

Laser Lipo body sculpting is the latest Non invasive technological breakthrough in fat reduction. You can lose your unwanted inches without surgery and providing you an effective solution for stubborn, localized fat areas with no pain and minimal downtime. Using state-of-the-art Laser Like Lipo wavelength technology, this revolutionary treatment gently “melts” fat to help eliminate it from your body! After treatment, the unwanted fat is naturally removed from the body through your digestive and lymphatic systems. You can treat fat on almost any area of your body with Non Invasive Laser Lipo body sculpting. The most commonly treated areas with Laser Lipo are: thighs, upper arms, love handles, chin, neck, buttocks, back and stomach. Treatment times with Laser Lipo will vary depending on what are you are treating.

Non Invasive Laser Lipo body sculpting can help you trim unwanted inches and can also be used to:

Reduce appearance of stretch marks
Sculpt and Contour “problem areas” of your the body
Reduce pain and inflammation
Reduce cellulite

Full treatment is usually completed in 12-20 visits, but many can see some improvement after just a few sessions. Laser Lipo treatments are non-invasive and pain-free!
